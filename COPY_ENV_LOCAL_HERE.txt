Your .env.local file must go in THIS folder, beside package.json.

Windows PowerShell:
  Copy-Item .env.example .env.local

Then fill in at least:
  NEXT_PUBLIC_SUPABASE_URL=https://YOUR_PROJECT.supabase.co
  NEXT_PUBLIC_SUPABASE_ANON_KEY=YOUR_ANON_KEY

Save the file, stop the development server, and run:
  npm run dev

Do not put .env.local in the outer Downloads folder.
Do not rename it to .env.local.txt.
